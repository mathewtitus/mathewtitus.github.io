Research

These are problems I'm interested in.

Stochastic processes

This paper by Sourav Chatterjee describes a stochastic process whose expectation solves the wave equation. The solution is not available for all domains and initial conditions.

Isospectral problem

Steven Zelditch of NWU has working notes on the eigenfunction problem on Riemannian manifolds. These are relevant to recent advances in the isospectral problem widely popularized by Mark Kac.

Fractals generated by dispersion equations

Some investigations into basic ODEs by Peter Olver has shown interesting self-similar behavior in the solutions to dispersion equations on \(S^1\).
