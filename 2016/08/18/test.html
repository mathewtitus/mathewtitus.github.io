Though I entered high school with no close friends with some passing sports involvement and having formed a tight circle of friends I left high school as something of an icon to lower classmen, an academic leader (albeit with two Bâ€™s ousting me from valedictorian/salutatorian status - from study hall and geometry, classes which required a certain amount of accountability, or as I saw it, self-directed busy work).
